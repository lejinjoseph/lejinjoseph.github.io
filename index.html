Guidelines for attending Holy Mass online
  1. Start preparing for holy mass before-hand. do not rush.
  2. Set-up a simple altar with a crucifix & a candle in your table near the device (mobile phone, tablet, laptop, tv) streaming the holy mass.
  3. Wear appropriate attire (do not attend the mass in your leisure wear or home wear). Be serious in your preparation.
  4. Turn off other online devices & do not do anything else while you are participating in the holy mass. there should be nothing to distract your attention on the mass.
  5. Recite the corresponding responses to the prayers in the holy mass.
  6. Observe the proper gestures of standing, kneeling & sitting at appropriate times in the holy mass.
  7. During the time of receiving communion, please recite the spiritual communion prayer.
